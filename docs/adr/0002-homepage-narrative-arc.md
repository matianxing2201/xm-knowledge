# Homepage Narrative Arc: Proof-First, Trending And Recent Merged, Roadmap Demoted

The reworked homepage follows a Hook → Proof → Navigate → Depth → Person arc, replacing the legacy flat order (Domain grid → Roadmap → Latest → Statistics at the bottom).

Section order, top to bottom:
1. Navbar (with persistent search trigger)
2. Hero — rich left column + interactive Knowledge Graph (right)
3. Statistics + Contribution Heatmap — promoted to immediately after Hero to prove "actively maintained"
4. Domains — presented with visual hierarchy, not a flat 2×2 grid
5. Trending + Recent Updates — merged into one section with a Hot/New tab toggle (avoids two similar lists back-to-back)
6. Knowledge System / Roadmap — demoted to carry the personal/systematic-thinking story
7. Footer

Reversing the old order matters because statistics and the heatmap are the strongest "this is alive" signals and were buried at the bottom. Merging Trending and Recent avoids two visually redundant card grids and makes the tab toggle itself a point of interaction.

Rejected: keeping current order and only strengthening weak sections — leaves the "alive" proof at the bottom and offers no integration point for the new Trending concept.
